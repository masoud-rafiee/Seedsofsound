Engineered by the 5-EYES

We, the 5-EYES of the Bishop's University Computer Science Club — Masoud Rafiee, Daniel James A. Carone, Umba Lubembele, Hugo Zamora Zorrilla, and Ineza Bonté — are the creators and engineers behind Seeds of Sound.

🔧 Full-Stack Engineering

We designed, built, and programmed the entire system from scratch — crafting all software, hardware, and firmware to transform vintage rotary telephones into interactive storytelling devices.

🎛️ Custom Embedded Systems

Each phone houses a custom Raspberry Pi-based embedded system, running original proprietary code we developed to control playback, interface logic, and sensor detection.

🌍 From Campus to Rome

Dr. Vivian Valencia, together with Masoud Rafiee and Imen Djendli, will present The First Supper — featuring Seeds of Sound — at the World Food Forum 2025 in Rome.

When the handset is lifted, the device plays a curated mosaic of voices — students, farmers, athletes, and dreamers — sharing stories about food, memory, and the future. Our engineering turns abstract ideas into living, tactile experiences, demonstrating how Computer Science and creativity can humanize technology.

Technical Documentation

⚡ Systems Setup

Purpose: A vintage rotary telephone, re-engineered with a Raspberry Pi Zero 2 W, plays audio recordings from an onboard archive when the receiver is lifted.

  • Connect USB-C power cable to 5V 2A adapter
  • Wait for startup beep confirmation
  • Lift receiver to trigger random recording
  • Hanging up instantly stops playback

🔧 Troubleshooting

  • No beep: Check USB-C plug, use 5V 2A charger
  • No sound: Firmly seat handset on/off switch
  • Static/distortion: Check speaker terminals, adjust gain
  • Won't start: Re-flash image from backup microSD

📡 Bluetooth Protocol

For file transfer & updates:

  • Open rear panel for mini HDMI port
  • Connect display and keyboard
  • Enable Bluetooth via terminal
  • Transfer audio to /home/pi/audio/
  • Supported: .wav, .mp3 (44.1 kHz)

🛡️ Maintenance Tips

  • Keep vent holes clear for cooling
  • Power down gently by unplugging only after 5 seconds of silence
  • Use microfiber cloth for cleaning exterior
  • Store indoors between 5°C and 35°C
  • Bluetooth disabled by default during exhibit hours for security
