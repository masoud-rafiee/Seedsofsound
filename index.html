Hardware & Circuit Engineering

Behind-the-scenes look at the circuit design, wiring, prototyping, and hardware integration that powers Seeds of Sound.

‚ö° Systems Setup

Purpose: A vintage rotary telephone, re-engineered with a Raspberry Pi Zero 2 W, plays audio recordings from an onboard archive when the receiver is lifted.

  • Connect USB-C power cable to 5V 2A adapter
  • Wait for startup beep confirmation
  • Lift receiver to trigger random recording
  • Hanging up instantly stops playback

üîß Troubleshooting

  • No beep: Check USB-C plug, use 5V 2A charger
  • No sound: Firmly seat handset on/off switch
  • Static/distortion: Check speaker terminals, adjust gain
  • Won't start: Re-flash image from backup microSD

üì° Bluetooth Protocol

For file transfer & updates:

  • Open rear panel for mini HDMI port
  • Connect display and keyboard
  • Enable Bluetooth via terminal
  • Transfer audio to /home/pi/audio/
  • Supported: .wav, .mp3 (44.1 kHz)
