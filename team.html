Our Team

Collaboration across disciplines brings Seeds of Sound to life

Meet the makers

Seeds of Sound is powered by a passionate group of students and mentors. Our core team is anchored by the Bishop’s University Computer Science Club, whose members engineered and programmed the audio installation. We collaborated closely with students and faculty from sustainable agriculture and food systems, arts administration, drama and psychology to ensure the project resonates across disciplines.

Behind the project

Here’s a look at our team during one of our build sessions. We come from diverse backgrounds but share a common goal: using technology and creativity to inspire change.

Acknowledgements

We extend our gratitude to the Bishop’s University Arts Festival for providing a platform to test these ideas, and to all the students, faculty, farmers and community members who contributed their voices. Special thanks to our mentors and advisors, whose guidance helped transform a simple concept into an immersive installation.
