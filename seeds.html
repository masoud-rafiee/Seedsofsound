Seeds of Sound Installation

An evocative archive of voices and memories housed within a repurposed rotary phone.

Installation Overview

Seeds of Sound invites visitors to "pick up the archive" of collective voices, memories and food reflections. This interactive sculpture uses a vintage rotary phone connected to a Raspberry Pi to play random recordings from our community archive. The technology is hidden inside the telephone, preserving its nostalgia while delivering crisp, highâ€‘fidelity sound.
